About / Bio

Intentional imperfection within a precise system.

I'm Jake McCormick, a multidisciplinary designer, visual artist, and musician based in Lexington, KY. My work explores the intersection of strict typographic grids and the tactile chaos of print making. Coming from a DIY music background, I believe in creating work that feels alive.

Whether I'm setting type for a corporate report or ripping screen prints for a local band, my approach remains grounded in structure, hierarchy, and a refusal of unnecessary decoration. Every element earns its place.
